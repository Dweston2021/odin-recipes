Chocolate Chip Cookies

Dan's Infamous Chocolate Chip Cookies

Do you have 30 minutes and a desire to shovel hot cookies into your mouth? Well look no further because I've got just the recipe for you. Follow my simple instructions and you'll be slamming hot goodness into your face hole in no time.

Ingredients

  • 2 Sticks Softened Butter
  • 1 1/2 Cups Sugar
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 2 1/4 Cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1 Package Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 375 degrees F
  2. Beat together butter, sugar, vanilla, eggs, and salt in a large bowl
  3. Add flour and baking soda to wet ingredients and mix
  4. Mix chocolate chips into dough
  5. Place 1oz balls of dough on ungreased baking sheet
  6. Bake for 9-12 minutes
  7. Remove from oven and place on cooling rack for five minutes
  8. Commence shoveling into face hole
